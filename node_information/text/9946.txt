Que savez-vous de la Préhistoire ?

   Inscrivez-vous gratuitement à notre cours en ligne pour découvrir le
   temps des chasseurs-collecteurs !
   Vous aborderez la Préhistoire à partir d’éléments concrets (sites

   véhiculés aujourd’hui !

   A la Réunion nous accompagnons la deuxième Maison Digitale de l’île
   avec l’association Runfablab. Cette formation numérique de 13 semaines

   évoluer au sein de ma nouvelle entreprise ! "

Un FabLab Solidaire ouvre ses portes à Montreuil !

   Le FabLab Solidaire La Verrière à Montreuil est un lieu d’échanges et
   de création numérique ouvert à tous.
   Les jeunes y sont formés à l’utilisation des outils de fabrication
   numérique et ils utilisent ensuite leurs connaissances pour concevoir
   des objets à destination des personnes souffrant d’un handicap.

   22 juillet 2019
   Agir pour les personnes avec autisme

Inauguration d’un FabLab Solidaire pour et par des jeunes handicapés

   Nous inaugurons un FabLab Solidaire un peu particulier au sein de
   l’Institution JB Thiery, dans une ancienne chapelle. Au FabLab « Les
   Mac Gyver » les jeunes autistes du centre pourront apprendre à
   fabriquer des pièces de rechange pour les fauteuils des enfants
   polyhandicapés. Le FabLab sera ainsi un pôle de formation pour les
   résidents afin de leur donner une compétence dans les métiers des
   nouvelles technologies. L’équipe du FabLab Solidaire de Champagnole
   (Jura) forme et accompagne les intervenants.

   5 juillet 2019

   Working for the future of young people

A Solidarity FabLab in Le Puy en Velay!

   In the Haute-Loire region, we support the Solidarity FabLab at the 2nd
   Chance School in Le Puy en Velay, which teaches students about 3D
   printing.
   The training manager encourages students to build small objects.
   “It’s different from your average training session. The students all
   get to take something home with them,” explains the school’s director.

   4 September 2019
   Working for the future of young people

A Solidarity FabLab opening in Montreuil!

   La Verrière Solidarity FabLab in Montreuil is a place for exchanging
   and digital creation open to all.
   Young people learn how to use digital manufacturing tools, allowing
   them to design objects for people with disabilities.

   22 July 2019
   Taking action for people with autism

Inauguration of a Solidarity FabLab for and by young disabled people

   A unique Solidarity FabLab is being opened at the Institution JB
   Thierry in a former chapel. At the “Mac Gyver” FabLab, young people
   with autism can learn to make spare parts for wheelchairs used by
   children with various disabilities. The FabLab will serve as a training
   centre for residents giving them skills in new technology. The
   Solidarity FabLab team in Champagnole, Jura, will train and support
   those who run the centre.

   2 July 2019

   We met with the women volunteers and team from the Digital Centre at
   Cité Vélane in Toulouse with Christine Albanel. The team explained,
   “They feel free, independent, and integrated [...] They help one
   another, and it forges bonds!”

Digital workshops for female prisoners in Mulhouse

   We support the Digit’elles project by Face Alsace in Mulhouse,
   providing female prisoners with IT training. In September 2019,
   twenty-four women received training to break the digital divide and
   encourage rehabilitation.

   The Women’s Digital Centre in Mayotte supports women to learn the

   watchwords of this 6-week computer literacy course.

   Solidarity FabLab program

After the Ongola FabLab… the mobile FabLab!

   On 23 May, we opened the first mobile Solidarity FabLab in Cameroon in
   association with AUF.
   Young Cameroonians in all regions of the country will be able to access
   digital manufacturing tools, develop their skills and make their
   projects a reality.

   28 May 2019
   Culture

And the winner of the Orange Book prize in Africa is...

   Cameroonian writer Djaïli Amadou Amal has won the Orange Book Award in
   Africa for his book "Munyal, the tears of patience", published by
   Proximité in 2017. The jury described the novel as “A strong, sincere,
   rebellious voice, served by a language that promotes its culture.” The
   Award launched in October 2018 reflects our desire to work to promote
   literary talent in Africa and encourage the local publishing industry.

   22 May 2019
   Digital Education

The Jastrzębie-Zdrój Women’s Digital Centre

   The Jastrzębie-Zdrój Digital Centre in Poland welcomes young women to
   develop their digital skills. The association runs "design thinking"
   workshops, logo creation or website creation, as well as professional
   workshops on how to create and manage your own micro company and
   prepare for a job interview.

   Taking action for people with autism

Inauguration of a Solidarity FabLab for and by young disabled people

   A unique Solidarity FabLab is being opened at the Institution JB
   Thierry in a former chapel. At the “Mac Gyver” FabLab, young people
   with autism can learn to make spare parts for wheelchairs used by
   children with various disabilities. The FabLab will serve as a training
   centre for residents giving them skills in new technology. The
   Solidarity FabLab team in Champagnole, Jura, will train and support
   those who run the centre.
